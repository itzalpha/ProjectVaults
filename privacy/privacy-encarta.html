Neo Encarta Kids

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 24, 2025  ·  Last Updated: July 24, 2025
A note for parents and guardians Neo Encarta Kids is made for children. Before your child can use the app, we ask for your consent. Please read this policy carefully — it tells you exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and what you can do about it at any time.

VerseCity Technology built Neo Encarta Kids as a safe educational space for children. We know that privacy matters more when children are involved, so we keep what we collect to the minimum that actually helps the app work well.

What We Collect

We collect six pieces of information when a child signs up, with a parent or guardian's permission:

  • Full name — so we can address the child properly throughout the app.
  • Nickname — for a friendlier in-app experience, displayed instead of the full name where appropriate.
  • Email address — used for account access and for sending progress updates to parents.
  • Age — so we can make sure the content shown is suitable for the child's stage.
  • Country — to deliver content relevant to where the child is, and to follow local privacy laws.
  • Gender — for personalising certain parts of the learning experience.

Nothing else. We do not track activity, store quiz results on our servers, or collect any device information.

How We Use It

Everything we collect serves one purpose: making the learning experience work better for the child. Specifically:

  • The name and nickname are used for personalised greetings and interactions inside the app.
  • The email address is used to manage the account and to keep parents informed about updates or progress.
  • Age is used to filter content so it is always age-appropriate.
  • Country helps us show locally relevant educational material.
  • Gender is used for lightweight personalisation within the app interface.

We do not use any of this information for advertising. We do not build marketing profiles. We do not show children ads.

Parental Consent

We will not create an account for a child under 13 without a parent or guardian first giving clear consent. If we ever discover that a child's information was collected without proper consent, we delete it immediately.

What Parents Can Do

As a parent or guardian, you have full control over your child's information. You can:

  • Ask to see everything we have stored about your child
  • Ask us to correct any information that is wrong
  • Ask us to delete your child's account and all associated data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

Any of the above can be done by emailing us at the address below. We will respond and act within 30 days.

Sharing Data

We do not sell children's data. We do not share it with advertisers or social media platforms. The only circumstance in which we would share information is if we are required to by law, or if VerseCity Technology is acquired — in which case parents would be notified beforehand.

How Long We Keep It

We keep your child's information for as long as the account is active. If an account is inactive for 12 months, we delete the data. Parents can also request immediate deletion at any point by contacting us.

Security

We take standard technical precautions to protect the data we hold. Access to children's information inside our systems is strictly limited. No method of storage is completely risk-free, but we do what we reasonably can.

Changes to This Policy

If anything meaningful changes, we will let parents know through the app and update this page. For changes that significantly affect how children's data is handled, we may ask for consent again.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Where applicable, we also follow COPPA (United States) and GDPR requirements for child users in those regions.
