🛡️ IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR PARENTS AND GUARDIANS

Encarta Kids (Neo Encarta) is designed for children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable children's privacy laws. We require parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13.

VerseCity Technology ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Encarta Kids (Neo Encarta) mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from children and their parents/guardians.

COPPA Compliance Statement

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13 without proper consent, we will delete it immediately.

With appropriate parental consent, we collect the following information:

For Children Under 13: We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any of this information. Parents can provide consent through our secure verification process.

2. How We Use Your Child's Information

We use the collected information solely for the following educational purposes:

  • Personalized Learning: Creating customized educational content based on age and learning preferences
  • Progress Tracking: Monitoring educational progress and achievements
  • Age-Appropriate Content: Ensuring content is suitable for the child's age group
  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining user accounts
  • Parental Communication: Sending progress reports and important updates to parents
  • App Improvement: Enhancing the educational experience (using anonymized data only)
We Never: Use children's information for advertising, marketing to children, or behavioral advertising. We do not create profiles for commercial marketing purposes.

3. Parental Rights and Controls

Parents and guardians have the following rights:

  • Review: Access all personal information collected from your child
  • Delete: Request deletion of your child's personal information
  • Refuse: Refuse further collection or use of your child's information
  • Consent Management: Withdraw consent at any time
  • Progress Reports: Receive regular updates on your child's learning progress

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade children's personal information. We may share information only:

  • With parents/guardians (progress reports, account information)
  • With service providers who assist in app functionality (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect safety
  • With explicit parental consent for specific purposes
Third-Party Services: We do not integrate with social media platforms or allow children to share personal information publicly.

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement robust security measures specifically designed for children's data:

  • Encryption of all personal information in transit and at rest
  • Restricted access to children's data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure servers with industry-standard protection
  • No data retention beyond what's necessary for educational purposes

6. Data Retention

We retain your child's information only as long as necessary to provide educational services or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Information retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • Parent Request: Immediate deletion upon parental request
  • Age Transition: Different policies may apply when child turns 13

7. International Data Transfers

If we transfer your child's data internationally, we ensure:

  • Adequate protection equivalent to this privacy policy
  • Compliance with applicable children's privacy laws
  • Appropriate safeguards and security measures

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We will notify parents directly of any material changes to this policy. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance of changes. For significant changes affecting children's privacy, we may require renewed parental consent.

9. Educational Purpose Statement

Encarta Kids (Neo Encarta) is designed solely for educational purposes. All data collection and processing supports legitimate educational activities and child development. We are committed to creating a safe, educational environment for children.
