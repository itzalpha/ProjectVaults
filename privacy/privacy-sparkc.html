Spark C

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 4, 2025  ·  Last Updated: August 4, 2025

At VerseCity Technology, we built Spark C as a tool people can actually trust. Part of that trust is being straightforward about what information we collect and why. So this document will just tell you exactly that — plainly, without unnecessary legal padding.

What We Collect

We only ask for four things when you sign up:

  • Your name — so we can address you properly inside the app.
  • Your email address — for account management, support, and occasional updates.
  • Your country — to understand where our users are and to comply with applicable local laws.
  • Your gender — for basic personalization within the app experience.

That is the full list. We do not collect anything else.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect your game results, quiz responses, psychological test outcomes, device identifiers, location data, or anything else from your activity inside the app. What you do in Spark C stays on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Your name and email are used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send you updates about the app when something meaningful changes
  • Occasionally share educational content around psychology and mental wellness

Your country and gender are used only to improve how the app feels for different users. We do not sell either of them.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or hand over your personal information to anyone — full stop. The only exceptions would be if we are legally required to (for example, by a court order), or if VerseCity Technology is ever acquired by another company, in which case you would be notified in advance.

How We Store It

We use standard security practices to keep your data safe. No system is perfect, but we take reasonable care of what you give us. Your information is kept for as long as your account is active. If you want it deleted, just reach out and we will take care of it within 30 days.

Your Rights

You can, at any time:

  • Ask to see what information we have on you
  • Ask us to correct anything that is wrong
  • Ask us to delete your data entirely
  • Withdraw your consent for us to process your data

Just send an email to the address below and we will sort it out.

Children

Spark C is not designed for anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we ever discover we have done so by mistake, we delete it immediately.

Third-Party Links

If the app links out to any external website, their privacy policies are their own — this one does not cover them.

Changes to This Policy

If something significant changes, we will update this page and notify you through the app. Continued use after a change means you are okay with it.

Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
