Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use Gift Verse:

Information You Provide Directly
  • Account registration details (name, email address, password)
  • Profile information you choose to add
  • Payment or wallet funding information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Communications and feedback you send to us
  • Wallet funding and transaction history (amounts funded, vouchers redeemed)
  • Lucky draw participation records and reward redemption history
  • Gift recipient details (name, contact) when you send digital gifts to others
  • Business account information (for B2B partners generating reward codes)
Information Collected Automatically
  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (features accessed, session duration, in-app behaviour)
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps, crash reports)
  • Location data (general, non-precise location derived from IP address)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, maintain, and improve Gift Verse features and functionality
  • Process transactions and deliver digital content or rewards
  • Send you service-related communications and updates
  • Personalise your experience within the app
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorised activity
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Facilitate lucky draws, cashback crediting, and reward redemptions
  • Enable digital gifting between users
  • Provide businesses with reward code generation and campaign analytics
  • Maintain cashback wallet balances and Gift Store transaction records

Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We work with trusted third-party partners who help us operate our platform (e.g. payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics providers). These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information where required by law, court order, or government authority.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of VerseCity Technology, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

With Your Consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure servers, and restricted staff access to personal data.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Gift Verse is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us at privacy@versecitytechnology.com.

Reward Codes & Business Partners

If you are a business partner using Gift Verse to distribute reward codes, you are responsible for ensuring that your customers are informed about how their data is used in connection with your loyalty campaigns. Cashback balances held in a user's Gift Verse wallet are not transferable, do not accrue interest, and have no cash value outside of the Gift Verse platform.
