The Luck Trap

Experience firsthand why the odds are stacked against you in games of chance. This interactive demonstration shows the mathematical reality behind gambling and helps you understand probability in an intuitive way.

Serial Memory Test

Discover the "serial position effect" - why you naturally remember the first and last items in a list better than those in the middle. Test your own memory patterns and learn how this affects everything from studying to presentations.
